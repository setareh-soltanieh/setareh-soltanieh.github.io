About Me

Hi, I’m Setareh Soltanieh – a Computer Vision Engineer and AR Developer passionate about building immersive, real-world applications.

With a Master’s in Electrical and Computer Engineering, I specialize in blending augmented reality, machine learning, and 3D reconstruction to create cutting-edge solutions that bridge the virtual and physical worlds. My research explores localization, perception systems, and real-time rendering—using tools like Unity, ROS, Python, and C++.

I’ve developed full AR pipelines, integrated NeRF models into VR, and worked across mobile and embedded systems to push the limits of what’s possible in spatial computing. Whether it’s synchronizing ROS topics with Unity, building VR demos for the Meta Quest, or comparing localization methods for urban planning, I’m always looking to turn bold ideas into practical impact.

Driven, curious, and committed to innovation, I thrive at the intersection of engineering, design, and user experience—helping people see the future before it’s built.
