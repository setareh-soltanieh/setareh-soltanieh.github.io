About Me

I operate at the intersection of AR/VR and AI.

As a developer specializing in AR/VR and AI, I am passionate about bridging the gap between the real and virtual worlds. My work focuses on bringing 3D objects to life while leveraging the power of AI to create innovative, intelligent, and immersive experiences.

I recently earned my Master’s degree from Queen’s University, where I explored cutting-edge projects at the intersection of AR, ML, and AI. My academic journey began at the University of Tehran, where I built a strong foundation in technology and problem-solving.

Whether it's designing immersive AR/VR applications or crafting AI-driven solutions, I am driven by a commitment to push the boundaries of innovation and contribute to shaping a more interconnected and intelligent future.
